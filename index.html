We are a Function Band in Devon

Hot Tin Ruth is a premium rock/pop covers band providing quality live entertainment for pubs, clubs and functions

Based in the heart of Torbay, our band specialises in delivering dynamic performances that span the decades, from the swinging 60s to today's chart-topping hits. With a repertoire bursting with rock, pop, and soul classics, we guarantee to keep your dance floor packed from the moment we strike our first chord until the final encore.

While Hot Tin Ruth may be a new name on the scene, our talented musicians bring years of individual experience to the stage, ensuring a polished and professional performance every time. Whether you're grooving to the iconic sounds of U2, getting funky with Prince, or rocking out to the Foo Fighters, our diverse setlist promises something for everyone. From intimate pub gigs to lively club nights, weddings, corporate events, and private parties, we're here to turn your special occasion into an unforgettable celebration.

Ready to book the hardest working band in Devon? Contact us today using the form at the bottom of the site, and let's make some music magic together!
