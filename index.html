Live music for music lovers

Hot Tin Ruth is a premium rock/pop covers band providing quality live entertainment for pubs, clubs and functions

Based in the heart of Torbay, our band specialises in delivering dynamic performances that span the decades, from the swinging 60s to today's chart-topping hits. With a repertoire bursting with rock, pop, and soul classics, we aim to keep your dance floor packed from the moment we strike our first chord until the final encore.

Our talented musicians bring years of individual experience to the stage, ensuring a polished and professional performance every time. Whether you're grooving to the iconic sounds of U2, getting funky with Prince, or rocking out to the Foo Fighters, our diverse setlist promises something for everyone. From intimate pub gigs to lively club nights, weddings, corporate events, and private parties, we're here to turn your special occasion into an unforgettable celebration.

Ready to book the hardest working band in Devon? Contact us today using the form at the bottom of the site, and let's make some music magic together!

The Band

Ruth / Vocals

From Dubai to Cuba and many places in between, Ruth has performed with a variety of bands and artists: pop, rock, funk & soul; Buddy Holly and Abba, Joan Jett and Joni Mitchell, Blondie, Pink, Queen, Handel and Mozart! As well as being a published song writer, she has performed on other artists' recordings and shows, and in theatre, but the one constant over the years of a varied music journey has always been fronting GREAT fun-time party bands in London and the South West. Tweaks in Torbay, The Riverview in Bristol and The Hard Place Band in Okehampton are examples. Basically, there's nothing that can quite beat getting brilliant local pubs and venues shaking with the people dancing like cats on a hot tin roof!

John / Drums

John is an experienced drummer who has been performing all over the South West playing pubs, clubs, conference centres and holiday parks. Over the last 10 years he has played for 'FAB' - a 60's band and a party band with Ruth called 'The Tweaks'. He has depped for various bands from a ten piece Soul band to Rock and Funk bands.

Jon / Guitar

Jon has played guitar in various bands and duos throughout Devon and London. He has always had an interest in the technical aspects of music and guitar, drawing inspiration from guitarists such as The Edge of U2, Francis Dunnery of It Bites and David Rhodes from the Peter Gabriel band. He has a keen interest in sound engineering and can often be found working in his home studio in Newton Abbot.
